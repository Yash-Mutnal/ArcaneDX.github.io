Comprehensive Laboratory Consulting

Arcane Dx possesses a team of highly skilled medical laboratory consultants in Clinical Pathology areas and other professionals dedicated to assisting you in managing and operating your laboratory efficiently. Whether you are initiating a new medical laboratory or aiming to expand your current test menu, we provide strategic solutions to enhance the success of your laboratory.

How We Can Assist

The selection of necessary instrumentation and qualified personnel is essential for establishing an effective and operational test menu in your laboratory. Arcane Dx will provide clinically relevant information and reimbursement data so you can make an informed decision about what is most suitable for your needs.

Whether this is your first laboratory experience, or you are expanding your existing test menu, the team of professionals at Arcane Dx has the expertise to custom tailor a test menu that aligns with your specialty or region of the country.

Ongoing CLIA Consulting and Regulatory Support

We offer on-going consulting and support with a monthly retainer fee. Our team is available five days a week for technical support, troubleshooting, and discussions about test menus and product development. We are committed to your lab's success.

Growth Strategy for Esoteric Test Menu

Expanding your laboratory's esoteric testing capabilities involves making informed decisions about introducing new and specialized tests. Part-Time Medical Lab Director Services

Our Part-Time Medical Lab Director Services are ideal for small labs and startups dealing with monthly reporting and regulatory compliance. Arcane Dx Lab Directors are HHS approved; board-certified professionals dedicated to your lab's success.

Our Part-Time Medical Lab Directors will:

  • Review and approve your SOPs
  • Sign off on monthly quality control logs
  • Perform all necessary duties in accordance with CLIA regulations

Directors support your lab with a combination of remote and on-site involvement. Most monthly tasks are remote, but your Lab Director will visit semi-annually, as mandated by CLIA, and be available for inspections as needed.

Laboratory Audits: Pre- and Post- Regulatory Inspections By CLIA, CAP, COLA, etc.

Our expert team ensures that your laboratory is thoroughly prepared for upcoming inspections, identifying areas of potential non-compliance and providing actionable recommendations. We conduct meticulous audits that align with the stringent standards of established regulatory bodies such as the College of American Pathologists (CAP), Clinical Laboratory Improvement Amendments (CLIA), and the Commission on Office Laboratory Accreditation (COLA).

We offer comprehensive pre-inspection audits designed to mirror the criteria and rigor of actual regulatory assessments. This proactive approach allows laboratories to address deficiencies and implement improvements well ahead of official evaluations. In the post- inspection phase, our audits serve to verify the effectiveness of corrective actions and sustain compliance, ensuring your laboratory continues to operate at the highest standards of quality and safety.

Partner with us to navigate the complexities of regulatory compliance, and maintain the integrity and excellence of your laboratory operations.
