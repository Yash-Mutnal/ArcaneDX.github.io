Our Services

Comprehensive Consulting in Clinical Immunology and Flow Cytometry

Diagnostic Immunology Consultation

Our expert team provides comprehensive diagnostic immunology consultation services to help you achieve clinical excellence.

  • Autoimmune Disease Panels: Comprehensive interpretation of autoimmune disease tests, covering screening, prognosis, monitoring, and single biomarker analyses to full disease panel evaluations.
  • Allergy Testing Guidance: Expert recommendations for allergen and complnent- specific IgE testing and evaluation.
  • Neuroimmunology Testing: Insights and guidance on plasma and CSF biomarker assays for Alzheimer’s disease and other neurodegenerative conditions.
  • Immunodeficiency Diagnostics: Detailed analysis of immunoglobulin levels and cellular immune responses for primary and secondary immunodeficiency disorders.

Flow Cytometry Services

Our specialized flow cytometry consulting helps you maximize the effectiveness of your cellular analysis operations.

  • Immunophenotyping: Expert support in identifying and characterizing immune cell populations./li>
  • Minimal Residual Disease (MRD) Analysis: Advanced assistance in monitoring hematological malignancies, including AML, CLL, ALL, and multiple myeloma.
  • Panel Design and Optimization: Customized flow cytometry panel development for research and clinical applications, such as CAR-T cell monitoring and biomarker development.
  • Data Analysis and Troubleshooting: Comprehensive analysis and troubleshooting to ensure precise and reliable results.

Laboratory Workflow Optimization

Streamline your laboratory operations with our workflow optimization expertise.

  • Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs): Development and review of SOPs for immunology and cytometry testing.
  • Instrument Validation and Maintenance: Ensuring optimal performance of flow cytometry instruments.
  • Compliance Support: Guidance for meeting CAP, CLIA, and ISO standards.

Education and Training

Enhance your team's knowledge and skills with our comprehensive education programs.

  • Workshops and Seminars: In-depth training for laboratory staff on immunology and flow cytometry techniques.
  • One-on-One Consultations: Personalized guidance to enhance skillsets and knowledge.
  • Educational Materials: Development of custom training guides and resources.
