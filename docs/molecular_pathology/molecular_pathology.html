Diagnostic Immunology Consultation

Our expert team provides comprehensive diagnostic immunology consultation services to help you achieve clinical excellence.

  • Autoimmune Disease Panels: Comprehensive interpretation of autoimmune disease tests, covering screening, prognosis, monitoring, and single biomarker analyses to full disease panel evaluations.
  • Allergy Testing Guidance: Expert recommendations for allergen and compliment-specific IgE testing and evaluation.
  • Neuroimmunology Testing: Insights and guidance on plasma and CSF biomarker assays for Alzheimer's disease and other neurodegenerative conditions.
  • Immunodeficiency Diagnostics: Detailed analysis of immunoglobulin levels and cellular immune responses for primary and secondary immunodeficiency disorders.

Flow Cytometry Services

Expert flow cytometry consultation for clinical diagnostics and research applications.

  • Panel Design and Optimization: Customized flow cytometry panel development for specific clinical and research applications.
  • Data Analysis Support: Advanced interpretation of complex flow cytometry data using cutting-edge analysis tools.
  • Clinical Validation: Assistance with validation of flow cytometry assays for clinical use.
  • Quality Control Programs: Implementation of robust QC procedures for flow cytometry laboratories.

Laboratory Workflow Optimization

Streamline your laboratory operations with our expert workflow consultation services.

  • Assay Development and Validation: Guidance on designing and validating molecular tests, including qPCR and NGS-based assays.
  • Automation and Instrumentation: Recommendations for adopting cutting-edge technologies to improve efficiency and accuracy.
  • Regulatory Compliance Support: Expertise in CAP, CLIA, FDA, and ISO standards to ensure compliance.
  • Data Management Solutions: Assistance with bioinformatics pipelines and molecular data analysis tools.

Education & Training

Comprehensive educational programs tailored for laboratory professionals.

  • Workshops and Seminars: Training on immunological diagnostic techniques and data interpretation.
  • Custom Training Programs: Tailored educational content for laboratory staff and clinicians.
  • Educational Materials: Development of training guides and SOPs for laboratory workflows.
  • Certification Preparation: Guidance for professional certification examinations in clinical immunology.
