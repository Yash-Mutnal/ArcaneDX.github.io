Our Specialized Services

Comprehensive Consulting Services in Clinical Chemistry and Toxicology

Clinical Chemistry Consultation

  • Laboratory Test Interpretation

    Detailed analysis of clinical chemistry panels, including renal function, liver function, cardiac markers, and endocrinology assessments.

  • Biomarker Guidance

    Selection and clinical correlation of biomarkers for specific diseases.

  • Laboratory Workflow Optimization

    Support for improving accuracy and efficiency in clinical lab processes.

Therapeutic Drug Monitoring (TDM)

  • Personalized Dosing Strategies

    Recommendations for optimizing therapeutic regimens for narrow therapeutic index drugs.

  • Clinical Efficacy and Toxicity Monitoring

    Expert correlation of drug levels with patient-specific clinical factors.

  • Training and Support

    Workshops for clinicians on therapeutic drug monitoring best practices.

Toxicology Consultation

  • Toxic Exposure Assessments

    Guidance on identifying and managing occupational, environmental, and accidental exposures.

  • Substance Abuse Testing

    Expertise in drug screening protocols and result interpretation.

  • Poison and Overdose Management

    Clinical recommendations for managing toxic ingestions and exposures, including antidote therapy.

Test Utilization and Method Validation

  • Test Optimization

    Assistance in reducing redundant testing while improving diagnostic outcomes.

  • New Assay Development

    Support for implementing and validating novel diagnostic assays in clinical settings.

  • Compliance Support

    Ensuring adherence to CAP, CLIA, and ISO standards for laboratory operations.
